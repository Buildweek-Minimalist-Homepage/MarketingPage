While having Google as your homepage may be a sensible choice, why not spice it up a little bit? The Nobuu Minimalist Homepage is exactly what you'd imagine it is: stylish, functional, minimalist and modern. Choose from a set of themes (light, dark, etc) and from a number of widgets (TBD). And of course, the Google search bar will be there too.
